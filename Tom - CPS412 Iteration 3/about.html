About.



Cypress is an automated web app designed to help the people of Toronto track and report problems
they notice on the street, allowing the city to keep track of issues affecting everyday citizens. Cypress will allow citizens to pinpoint problems on a map
and accurately describe the problem. Citizens can also login to view problems in a specfic area.
